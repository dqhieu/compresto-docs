When you first launch Compresto, you'll be guided through a quick setup process.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Welcome">
    Press **Get Started** to begin the setup process.

    ![Welcome](/images/welcome.jpg)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Installation">
    Compresto will automatically download and install all required dependencies. This usually takes a few minutes.

    A progress bar shows the installation status. Once complete, press **Next** to continue.

    ![Installation](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at10.38.47@2x.png)

    <Note>
    If installation fails, press **Try Again** to retry. Make sure you have an active internet connection.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="License Activation">
    Enter your license key and press **Activate** to activate Compresto.

    ![License](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at10.39.56@2x.png)

    <Info>
    **Don't have a license key?** Purchase one at [compresto.app/pricing](https://compresto.app/pricing)
    </Info>

    <Tip>
    **Setapp users**: Your license is automatically managed by Setapp. Simply press **Next** to continue.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    You're all set! Press **Continue** to start compressing files.

    ![Done](/images/done.jpg)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Gets Installed

During the installation step, Compresto downloads these components:

- **FFmpeg** - For video and GIF processing
- **Gifski** - For high-quality GIF creation
- **pngquant** - For PNG optimization
- **Ghostscript** - For PDF compression

These tools are installed in the app's support directory and don't affect your system.

## Troubleshooting

### Installation stuck or failed

1. Check your internet connection
2. Press **Try Again** to restart installation
3. If the issue persists, restart the app

### License activation failed

- Verify your license key is correct (format: XXXXXXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX-XXXXXXXXXXXX)
- Check that you haven't exceeded the activation limit
- Ensure you have an active internet connection

### Need to redo setup

If you need to go through the setup again:
1. Quit Compresto
2. Delete the app preferences (hold Option while clicking "Compresto" menu > "Reset Preferences")
3. Relaunch Compresto
