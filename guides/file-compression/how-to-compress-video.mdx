---
title: "How to compress video"
icon: 'video'
---

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click on the selection panel, or drop files/folders into it
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 10 45 51@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at10.45.51@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Adjust the video options for your desire output
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 10 47 39@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at10.47.39@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Press Compress to start compressing selected videos
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 10 47 58@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at10.47.58@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Once the compression finishes, you will be able to see the details of compressed videos. You can also click Open in Finder to open your video in Finder window
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 10 48 54@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at10.48.54@2x.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
