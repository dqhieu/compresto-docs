---
title: "How to compress image"
icon: 'image'
---

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click on the selection panel to select images, or drop your images/folders into it
    ![Image](/images/image.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Adjust the image options for your desire output
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 01 03@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.01.03@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Press Compress to start compressing your image
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 01 03@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.01.03@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Once the compression finishes, you will be able to see the details of compressed images. You can also click Open in Finder to open your images in Finder window
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 02 20@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.02.20@2x.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
