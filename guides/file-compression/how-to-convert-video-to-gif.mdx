---
title: "How to convert video to gif"
icon: 'arrow-right'
---

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click on the selection panel to select videos, or drop your videos/folders into it
    ![Image](/images/image.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Select video format as GIF, then adjust the gif options for your desire output
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 05 27@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.05.27@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Press Convert to start converting your video to gif
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 05 27@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.05.27@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Once the conversion finishes, you will be able to see the details of converted gif. You can also click Open in Finder to open your gif in Finder window
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 06 24@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.06.24@2x.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
