---
title: "How to compress PDF"
icon: 'file-pdf'
---

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Click on the selection panel to select pdfs, or drop your pdfs/folders into it
    ![Image](/images/image.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Adjust the pdf options for your desire output
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 10 10@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.10.10@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Press Compress to start compressing your pdf
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 10 10@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.10.10@2x.png)
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Once the compression finishes, you will be able to see the details of compressed pdf. You can also click Open in Finder to open your pdf in Finder window
    ![Clean Shot 2025 12 29 At 11 11 01@2x](/images/CleanShot2025-12-29at11.11.01@2x.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
